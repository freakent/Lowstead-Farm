Lowstead Farm Cottage is an ideal base for walking, exploring the history of the area or simply relaxing and enjoying the stunning views. Click on the links below to find out more.

Local Attractions Include

  • North Yorkshire Steam Railway - Levisham Station, located in the valley just one mile from the village, has a cafe and art gallery
  • Pickering - An attractive market town with three museums and ruined  castle.
  • Dalby Forest - Outdoor activities, bike hire, vistor centre, cafe and shops
  • Goathland - The beautiful village of Goathland, known to millions as "Aidensfield" in the ITV series "Heartbeat"
  • Castle Howard - One of Britain's finest historic houses
  • Ryedale Folk Museum - Take a step back to the 18th Century to find out both how and where we used to live
  • Eden Camp - Built in 1942 as a POW camp, now a fascinating WWII wartime museum
  • Farther afield - Whitby, Malton, Helmsley, York, Scarborough
